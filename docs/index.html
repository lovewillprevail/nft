Intro

A monthly NFT Competition, that aims at connecting NFT Creators, Collectors and Art lovers. Throughout the event, creators of all levels and mediums can submit their creations during a give timeframe. (specified on our Twitter account, each month)

Like most "artistic" creations, this event is a work in progress. An ever-evolving sketch. The rules, the jury, the concept, will evolve from month to month, based on the feedback, the ideas, the mistakes and lessons learnt.

Calendar

September 23rd to 24th - 7PM UST

During the 24 hour timeframe from September 23rd 7PM UST to September 24th 24th, NFT creators will be able to submit their creation.

Jury

The Jury of the contest is picked among members of the NFT Community. Jury members do not receive any form of payment. They benefit from increased exposure through the event. Their biography is available on our Jury page

They pride on being neutral, skilled and will select the artworks according to their personal feelings, with no intervention from the organizers.

Feel free to discover their profiles on the Jury Page

Rules

You can participate to the INFTC free of charge. No cookie / Googld Adsense information is gathered by our website. There are no financial prizes associated to winners. But for those who wish to support the initiative, donations or collections of #NFTs from organizers of Jury members are always welcome.
