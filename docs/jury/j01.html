Angelica Fuchs

Angelica Fuchs is a Swiss-based photographer and composite artist that captures pieces of reality brought together. She uses darker tones and mostly night lights to represent the dream state, as she aims to bring her imagination to reality in harmony with the world she interacts with. Her work represents extensions of truth with the ultimate goal of triggering self-interrogation on oneâ€™s perception. Her passion for the outdoor grew significantly in the past years, as she has found a way to creatively express her vision with photography. Her images are usually the result of days of hard work; preparing her adventures, hiking and sleeping in the mountains, and creating behind the screen to bring her creative vision to life.
