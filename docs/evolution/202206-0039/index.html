My artistic evolution

I am Lore Gardelle, born in Bordeaux and settled in Bilbao. After studying Art and Design at the Faculty of Fine Arts in Bilbao, I explored different techniques and styles until I found my own. I had always been passionated about photography, and during my degree, I discovered endless possibilities in abstraction to investigate. At first, I was more attracted to geometric black and white works.

Shortly after, the pandemic influenced the way I express myself, drawing me closer to colors and organic shapes, almost unconsciously. I found in abstract photography an oneiric world where to escape from reality and see the world from another point of view.

A way to immerse myself in my personal world, and to seek a connection with the receiver through feelings and sensations that appeal to the history of each one. From an introverted nature, abstract art is for me a way to express emotions and thoughts, and to bring them closer to others.
