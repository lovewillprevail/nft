My artistic evolution

It all began when I was young as I had an infatuation with reading and fantasy. I read dozens of books in a period of a few weeks and the local library became my home. I began picturing worlds in my head and it was as if I had lived there all my life. I started reading more, and as a result, I began writing as well. I always felt lacking because I wanted to visually show others the ideas in my head. It all came into the light a few years ago as I taught myself how to draw late at night at work. I began obsessed with doodling and sketching and it made me happy. Recently, I discovered AI notebooks and I was instantly in love. I combined my sketches with AI and I created pure randomness and surreal pieces of art. I was hooked! Now whatever comes into my head, is now put on paper. Drawn by hand and refined by AI. I wish to share my passion with the world and let others see my imagination firsthand.

What's next

My next big state of evolution is to become better at portraits and drawing human figures. It's my weakness, but I want to learn how. I have lore and landscapes to show worlds that I created, but I was never able to draw what the inhabitants looked like. My next big step is to learn how to create life!
